About

Professor Cesar Mello is a theoretical physicist specializing in spectral gravitation, black hole structure, and operator-based derivation of physical constants. He is an expert in the Einsteinian and Dirac formalisms, with deep command of quantum mechanics and functional methods. He holds a PhD in Theoretical Physics from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), and has over 40 years of academic experience.

Now retired from formal teaching due to a health condition—fully in remission, thanks to God—he is fully dedicated to foundational research at the Cosmo Physics Organization.
